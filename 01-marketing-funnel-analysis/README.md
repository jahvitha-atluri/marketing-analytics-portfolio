## Marketing Funnel & Conversion Analysis

### Business Objective
Analyze customer journey from website visit to inquiry to booking, identify drop-off points, and recommend actions to improve conversion efficiency.

### Dataset
Simulated marketing dataset representing sessions, inquiries, and bookings across multiple acquisition channels and devices.

### Tools Used
SQL, Excel, Power BI

### Analysis Performed
- Built a 3-stage funnel: Sessions → Inquiries → Bookings
- Calculated conversion rates by stage and channel
- Analyzed performance by device to identify UX friction
- Evaluated cost efficiency for paid channels

### Key Insights
- Largest drop-off occurred between Inquiry → Booking
- Organic and Referral channels showed higher booking conversion
- Mobile users showed higher drop-off at booking stage

### Business Recommendations
- Improve mobile booking UX to reduce friction
- Reallocate spend toward high-converting channels
- Introduce follow-up automation for inquiry-stage drop-offs
